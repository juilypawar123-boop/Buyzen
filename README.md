# Buyzen
Buyzen is a comprehensive, full-featured e-commerce and retail management software built with **.NET MAUI**, designed to streamline retail operations, enhance customer experience, and provide actionable business insights.

---

## **Key Features**

### **User Account & Personalization**
- User profiles with address book and payment management
- Account security overview and customizable communication preferences

### **E-Commerce Functionality**
- Product catalog with detailed descriptions, pricing, and categories
- Shopping cart with real-time price recalculation, quantity steppers, discount code input, and shipping estimator
- Wishlist to save preferred items with stock alerts and price change indicators

### **Order Management**
- Checkout & order confirmation with address selection, delivery method, and multi-step decision flow
- Order summary and tracking page with timelines, carrier info, ETA, and status history
- Returns & refunds page with policy summary, eligible items, and workflow-based requests
- Order management dashboard with filters, status updates, customer details, and fulfillment notes

### **Analytics & Business Insights**
- Sales & analytics dashboard with revenue charts, best-selling products, conversion metrics, and date-range selector
- Export reports and drill-down metrics for detailed insights

### **Administrative Features**
- Product management with inventory indicators, pricing editor, visibility toggle, and bulk actions
- Back-office controls for operational tasks including order status, courier assignment, refund management, and workflow orchestration

### **User Support**
- Integrated support & help center with FAQ, ticket creation, and live chat placeholder

### **UI/UX Highlights**
- Playful, colorful, and professional layouts for engaging user experience
- Icons and images for products and categories
- Multi-step, modular design for intuitive navigation
- Responsive and visually appealing interface across devices

---

## **Technology Stack**
- **Frontend & Backend:** .NET MAUI (C#)
- **Data Management:** ObservableCollections for in-memory data store
- **Persistence:** MAUI Preferences for user login state
- **UI Controls:** CollectionView, Picker, ScrollView, Frames, Buttons, and Editor
